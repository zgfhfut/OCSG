In the "harsh" condition, the ratio of agents to goals is 2:3.
In the "relax" condition, the ratio of agents to goals is 3:1.
In this experiment, the agents, goals, and interests are derived from “Zhaopin Su, Guofu Zhang*, Feng Yue, Jindong He, Miqing Li, Bin Li, Xin Yao. (2020) Finding the largest successful coalition under the strict goal preferences of agents. ACM Transactions on Autonomous and Adaptive Systems, 14(4) Article 15.”, while the costs and rewards are generated by us. A total of 30 sets were used.
